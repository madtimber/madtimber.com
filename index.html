My Services...

WHAT I DO

Web Development

I provide a wide range of development services, ranging from font-end development (HTML, CSS, Javascript), to middle tier coding (PHP, Java, C#/.NET) and back-end database persistence (MySQL, MS SQL). I’m also familiar with a number of web frameworks, including: .NET, Struts, Spring, CakePHP, Code Ignighter, jQuery, mooTools and prototype.

User Experience

During my ten years of web development/design (6 professionally), I’ve had the opportunity to work with some extraordinary people and companies (ExactTarget, ChaCha, Compendium Blogware, Tuitive). While with these companies, I’ve lead and mentored UI teams, designed and developed cutting edge software, managed offshore teams, and handled many aspects of project management.

Web Design

My design experience encompasses the entire iterative design life cycle: idea conception, requirements gathering, wire framing, prototyping, usability testing, hi-fi mockups. I believe that key to creating great web applications and products is to design well upfront, keeping the users needs at the forefront of every design decision and discussion.
